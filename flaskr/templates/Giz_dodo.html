Giz DoDo Recipe

GizDoDo is an African dish that is made with plantain, gizzard, and sauce. It is often eaten as a side-dish.

This recipe was adpted from Wikipedia and cheflolaskitchen.com. The image was gotten from sisijemimah.com.

Ingredients:

  • Ripe Plantain
  • Gizzard
  • Tomatoes
  • Broth
  • Bell Peppers(variety)
  • Onions
  • Scoth Bonnet
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Seasoning(Thyme, Salt, Chicken seasoning)

Kitchen Utensils:

  • Saucepan
  • pot
  • collander
  • Turning spoon
  • Chopping board and Knives
  • Serving spoon

Directions

Here are the steps for GizDoDo:

Step1:

Fry Gizzard and Plantain, to be able to do this you need to peel, cut, and fry plantain till golden brown. Wash gizzard thoroughly and cut gizzard into preffered size. Seasoon the gizzard and boil in a pot till well cooked. Then drain the pot and fry the gizzard

Step2:

Prepare the sauce, to get the sauce you need to preheat some oil in the saucepan. Then dice onions, the different bell peppers, tomatoes, and scoth bonnet. Pour them into saucepan and fry for about 5 minutes. If too thick, add broth.

Step3:

Add seasoning to current mixture in pot, then add the fried gizzard and plantain. Stir everything together thoroughly. Food is ready!
