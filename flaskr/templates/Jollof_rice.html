Jollof Rice Recipe

Jollof rice is rice dish from West Africa and different countres prepare it uniquely. This Jollof Rice Recipe is the Nigerian way of preparing it. Jollof Rice can be eaten with any side of choice and can be cooked to suit preferences

This recipe was adpted from Wikipedia and Seriouseats.com. The image was gotten from flawlessfood.com.

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes
  • Red Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Scoth Bonnet
  • Broth/ Water
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Seasoning(Bay Leaves, Curry, Thyme, Salt, Chicken seasoning)
  • White rice
  • Tomato paste
  • Garlic and Ginger(optional)

Kitchen Utensils:

  • Blender
  • Saucepan
  • Turning spoon
  • Pot
  • Chopping board and Knives
  • Measuring cup
  • Serving spoon

Directions

Here are the steps for Smoky Nigerian cooking Jollof rice:

Step1:

Get Stew Base by blending Tomatoes, Bell peppers, Onion, Scotch Bonnet, Garlic and Ginger if included and Broth together till the mix is properly blended.our blended mix into a saucepan and boil. Cover partially and boil on medium to low heat. Boil for about 20 minutes

Step2:

Get pot out and turn on heat, pour vegitable oil into pot and let it get hot.Pour chopped onions into hot vegetable oil and let it fry, then add tomato paste to pot and let that fry with the onions. After frying for about 5 minutes, add seasoning to put and stir thoroughly.

Step3:

Pour broth into pot and mix properly. Be careful with the quantity cause you don't want the broth to be more that rice so the food won't be soggy. Let that sit for about 2 mins. Wash rice and pour it into the pot. Stir rice in until it is evenly coated in sauce. Cover the pot properly with foil paper inorder to trap steam. Reduce to low heating and allow to cook for about 20 minutes. Check after 20 minutes and if rice is soft add little butter and stir properly. Food is ready
