Shrimp Alfredo Recipe

Fettuccine Alfredo is an italian pasta dish that can be cooked in different ways. This recipe would be about Shrimp Fettuccine Alfredo.

This recipe was adpted from Wikipedia and delish.com. The image was gotten from Nutmegnanny.com.

Ingredients:

  • Shrimp
  • Fetuccine
  • Heavy cream
  • Butter
  • Seasoning( Salt, Chicken seasoning, garlic powder, all purpose seasoning)
  • Whole milk
  • Egg yolk
  • water

Kitchen Utensils:

  • Saucepan
  • Turning spoon
  • Pot
  • Measuring cup
  • Serving spoon

Directions

Here are the steps for Shrimp Fettucine Alfredo:

Step1:

Cook fettuccine by pouring it inside a pot of boiling water. Directions on the fettuccine box can be followed. After boiling reserve a cup of pasta water.

Step2:

Wash and clean shrimps, after that season properly and fry in a saucepan with melted butter. Cook till it is properly done. Set shrimp aside

Step3:

Add more butter, garlic and seasoning to saucepan. Pour in heavy cream, milk and mix together with eggyolk. Reduce heat and mix with parmesan. Make sure cheese has melted and the sauce is thick. Add cooked fettucine and fried shrimps to saucepan. Put more seasoning to suit preference. Additional cheese can be added based on preference. Food is Ready
